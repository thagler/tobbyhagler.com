The Grove of Tau Ceti releases tonight

I’m thrilled to share that The Grove of Tau Ceti has officially launched!

This marks the end of a long and surreal journey — from drafting the first chapter beside a quiet Carolina pond to holding the finished book in my hands.

The novel is now available in paperback and Kindle on Amazon, with additional distributors coming soon. Other ebook formats will follow in late January.

You can learn more at the official website: thegroveoftauceti.com
