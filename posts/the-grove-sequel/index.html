The Grove of Tau Ceti Sequel

After months of putting it off, and working on short story after short story, I'm pleased to say that I'm finally back to working on the sequel to The Grove of Tau Ceti!

I will keep the update as spoiler-free of the first book as I can, but you may not want to continue if you haven't finished the first book yet.

A month passes after the events of the first book, and the crew has finally met up with their new allies, the Atharans and Kentaurans. Who, understandably, are more than a little upset that they haven't been able to contact their respective homeworlds since their arrival.

Needless to say, hilarity does not ensure when Captain John Hargreaves explains (mostly) the events that took place in the first novel. And the rest of the surviving crew is still on edge, for obvious reasons. Still, they have a mission to fullfill. 

That hyperspace beacon isn't going to build itself...

Stay tuned. This one will be out in Febrary 2027.
