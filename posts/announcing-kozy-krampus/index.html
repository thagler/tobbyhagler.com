Announcing Kozy Krampus

I'm more excited than a demon with a sack full of kids!

My first published short story will appear in Kozy Krampus, an anthology of 29 delightfully twisted tales featuring the horned holiday menace himself. The collection drops late September, just in time to curl up with a mug of something warm and a healthy fear of hooves on the rooftop.

So drop what you're doing and go pre-order now! Or don’t. That’s up to you. But remember: Krampus thinks disobedient children taste the best.

Mark Teppo and Frances Lu-Pai Ippolito, eds., Kozy Krampus (Portland, OR: Underland Press, 2025).
