New Project in the Works

I'm happy to announce that I have a new project in the works: Frankie and the Goblin King.

Frankie is a very good dog with a very bad problem. A deposed goblin king has taken up residence in his back leg, and when the King’s royal advice gets Frankie hauled into an animal shelter, the mismatched pair must learn to share four legs, outwit a ticking clock, and lead a pack of dogs toward a farm that may be more bedtime story than refuge.

I've been writing pieces of a novella about Frankie, a good but perhaps naïve dog who accidentally summons the deposed king of the Goblin realm, who happens to be looking for a place to hide. Frankie's broken leg seems like the perfect place, and the instant it heals, their bond is solidified. Neither can escape the other... and trust me, they would both love it if they could.

I've decided to commit the next few months to this cozy paranormal horror story, and I'll likely be finished with the first draft in the next 2-3 months.

I'm pretty excited about this one!
